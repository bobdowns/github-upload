Who I Am and How to Use This Portfolio

Hi! My name is Bob Downs. I'm a meticulous Web Designer. This website showcases what I've learned while earning my Associate in Applied Science Computer Technology degree, Certificate of Completion Computer Usage and Applications, and Certificate of Completion Web Design: User Interface from Rio Salado College in Tempe, Arizona.

It further showcases what I've learned while earning my Bachelor in Applied Science Internet and Web Development degree from Arizona State University in Tempe, Arizona.

Clicking on the menu items to the left will lead you to the various web design works I've created while earning the above degrees and certificates. And, the Resume menu item will show you my resume and make it available for download. These items will show you the breadth and depth of my knowledge in web design, web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery & AJAX), user interface design, user experience design, design principles like color theory, Gestalt Principles, as well as Adobe software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Animate (Flash), and After Effects.

Additional Things I've Learned

I've also taught myself to register domain names and set up web hosting at GoDaddy, Namecheap, Hostinger, and WPEngine. And, before I'd finished my associate's degree, I single-handedly converted a high school PTO's Microsoft Frontpage website into a WordPress website in 2012. I then greatly increased its utility by adding the ability for parents to donate to the PTO via PayPal. I developed a publishing schedule and proofread and edited various contributors' articles before posting them on the new website. Further, I developed and refined the PTO's Constant Contact weekly eNewsletter, increasing readership by 35%.
